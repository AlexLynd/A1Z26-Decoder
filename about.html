What is A1Z26?

A1Z26 is a simple cipher where A=1, B=2, C=3 ... Z=26. This simple cipher is also known as the Caesar Cipher (no shift), and is useful for simple encoding.

What is this website?

This website is an A1Z26 encoder/decoder and also offers a simple quiz service. This site was made as an exercise by Alex Lynd and Michael Lee.
